Our Story

More than
a gym.

The Base was born from a simple idea: in Ubud, people come from all over the world to slow down, reconnect, and find their rhythm. We built a place where you can train hard in the morning, open your laptop over a protein shake, and share a story with a stranger by noon.

No mirrors on every wall. No DJ music at 7am. Just solid equipment, cool air, good coffee, and a community that keeps showing up — from Bali locals to digital nomads passing through.

The Team

Our Coaches

A small team. A big welcome. They know your name on day one.

WAYAN
Head Coach · Strength & Conditioning

Born and raised in Ubud, Wayan has been training athletes and everyday people for over 8 years. He built The Base because he wanted a space where training felt real — not performative. His philosophy: consistency beats intensity, every time.

SARAH
