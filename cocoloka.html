Our Story

Born in the Heart
of Ubud

"For movers, lifters & mindful eaters — we believe food is the most powerful fuel."

Nestled on Jalan Raya Sangingan in the lush heart of Ubud, Cocoloka was born from a simple belief: that eating well should feel extraordinary. We craft every bowl, shake, and plate with whole, nutrient-dense ingredients — no shortcuts, no compromises.

Whether you're post-yoga, mid-hike, or simply seeking a nourishing breakfast, Cocoloka is your sanctuary. Our kitchen runs from dawn to late evening, so there's always a moment for something vibrant.
